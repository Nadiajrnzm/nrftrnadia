MY HOME FAMILY

My family consists of a mother, a father, two sisters and a brother. My two sisters are already married and have children. Only my brother and I are still unmarried. Here I will introduce my family members one by one.

Image Description
The first is my father. My father was named Mahmad Junsin bin Matayasin @ Mat Yasin. My father is 61 years old and comes from Beaufort, Sabah, Malaysia. He started migrating here when he was 18 years old. My father used to work in various sectors to support our family until my father worked as an assistant supervisor at a company in Subang. Moreover, now, my father is not working because he has reached the age of 60. Nevertheless, my father is a hard worker and decided to work as a cleaner in a shopping centre.
Second is my mother. My mother was named Norrizam Binti Ismail. My mother is 51 years old and comes from Klang, Selangor. My mother's hobby is embroidery. My mother also worked in various sectors to help my father support our four siblings' lives. His last job was as a production operator in a food production company, and now my mother is not working, but my mother decided to take care of my nephew.
My first sister is Noor Fatihah Binti Mahmad Junsin. My sister is 28 years old and works at Panasonic. In addition, my sister also deals in handicrafts. Kakak has a husband named Muhammad Syazwan and has two children named Muhammad Aneeq Naufal and Muhammad Aqeef Nufail, who are five years old and two years old.
My second sister was named Noor Fazliyatullyianah Binti Mahmad Junsin. My second sister is 26 years old and a housewife. My second sister helps my mother manage my nephew at home. My second sister has a husband named Muhd Azizi and a daughter named Nur Ayana Zafirah, who is only five months old.
